01. About

I work across business intelligence, operational analytics, and web/product builds.

At Lakshmikumaran & Sridharan, I work as a Data Consultant on internal dashboards and analytics workflows for matter/case performance, revenue indicators, lawyer-level performance, and leadership review.

Before that, I led school-program operations at Edudigm across assessments, STEM initiatives, LMS workflows, PM SHRI programs, and large-scale student activities. That work made one thing obvious: clarity breaks when evidence, ownership, and timelines live in too many places.

My current focus is to turn that complexity into business intelligence systems, dashboards, and trackers that are easier to read and easier to act on.

My foundation combines an M.Sc. in Mathematics from Jadavpur University with a 6th-year diploma in Fine Arts. The math gives me the structure. The art training keeps the final dashboard or page calm, balanced, and legible.

My Philosophy

Make the work easier to see.

01

The problem

Most teams are not short on effort. The harder problem is seeing the same facts in the same place: what changed, what needs attention, and what should happen next.

02

The method

I start with the decision the view needs to support. Who is looking at it? What are they comparing? What would make the next conversation sharper?

03

The result

That usually becomes a dashboard, tracker, assessment analysis, program report, or focused page. The format changes. The job stays the same: make the work easier to understand and use.
