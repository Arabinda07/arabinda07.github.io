01. About Me

I'm a mathematician and program strategist who crossed into data — and found that rigorous thinking transfers perfectly. Currently I'm a Data Consultant at Lakshmikumaran & Sridharan (LKS) in New Delhi, one of India's leading full-service law firms, where I build internal dashboards and tools, and work on Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023 compliance frameworks.

Before LKS, I spent three years at Edudigm as Program Head — building multi-school programs from scratch, designing government-aligned initiatives across Kendriya Vidyalayas, and scaling a national quiz competition to 600+ schools across 6 states. My work was featured at CBSE Sahodaya 2024.

I hold an M.Sc. in Mathematics from Jadavpur University (Graph Theory, Number Theory). That training is the backbone of how I approach every data and systems problem.
