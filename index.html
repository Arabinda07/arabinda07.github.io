01. About

My work sits where data meets daily operations: internal reviews, school programs, assessments, stakeholder updates, and the tables people depend on but rarely enjoy maintaining.

I work at the intersection of data, dashboards, and institutional operations.

At Lakshmikumaran & Sridharan, I work on confidential internal dashboards and decision views for matter/case performance, revenue indicators, lawyer-level performance, and leadership review.

Before that, I led school-program operations at Edudigm across assessments, STEM initiatives, LMS workflows, PM SHRI programs, and large-scale student activities. That work taught me how quickly institutional work becomes scattered, and how useful it becomes when the structure is visible.

My Philosophy

Making work easier to see.

01

Why

Most teams are not short on effort. The harder problem is seeing the same facts in the same place: what changed, what needs attention, and what should happen next.

02

How

I start with the decision the view needs to support. Who is looking at it? What are they comparing? What would make the next conversation sharper?

03

What

That usually becomes a dashboard, tracker, assessment analysis, program health view, or focused page. The format changes. The job stays the same: make the work easier to understand.
