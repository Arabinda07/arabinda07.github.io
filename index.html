01. My Journey

From mathematics to data systems — a path driven by structure and scale.

I started as a mathematician — trained to think in structure, abstraction, and rigour.

Mathematics→ Education→ Programs→ Systems→ Data

Then I moved into education, where I realised something quickly: most programs don't fail because of ideas. They fail because they don't scale.

At Edudigm, I spent three years building and operating multi-school systems — designing programs, working with Kendriya Vidyalayas, and scaling initiatives across states. I wasn't just teaching or managing — I was building systems that had to work in the real world.

That experience led me into data.

Today, as a Data Consultant at Lakshmikumaran & Sridharan (LKS), I work on dashboards, internal tools, and data governance frameworks — including work around the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023.

Across both domains, my focus remains the same: build systems that are structured, measurable, and scalable.
