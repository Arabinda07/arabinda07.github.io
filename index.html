From mathematics into systems work, shaped by what survives real institutional pressure.

At Edudigm, that habit moved into school programs, assessments, quizzes, and multi-school initiatives. The work had to survive timetables, teachers, budgets, student attention, and rollout pressure. Scale was not a slogan there; it was the everyday test.
