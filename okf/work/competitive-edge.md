# Competitive Edge — Diagnostic Assessment Analytics

Diagnostic assessment analytics work using Bloom's taxonomy and LOTS-HOTS
classification across about 1,000 students and about 10 schools, supporting
learning-gap identification, remediation planning, and school-level academic
feedback — helping teachers see where learning was breaking down and why. Case
study visibility through CBSE Sahodaya 2024.

By [Arabinda Saha](../arabinda-saha.md).
