# LKS Internal Performance Dashboard Work

Internal dashboard work at Lakshmikumaran & Sridharan (a national law firm),
described at a functional level: matter/case performance, revenue indicators,
lawyer-level performance indicators, and leadership review — built with SQL,
Python (Pandas/Plotly) and Power BI. Details are kept at a functional level; no
employer metrics, client data, or confidential internal details are disclosed.
